1. Data Kayi Collects

Kayi may collect personal identification data, contact details, addresses, identity verification information, transaction and account records, device/browser information, and usage logs required for service delivery and compliance.

  • Personal/profile details: name, email, phone number, address and related onboarding information.
  • Financial/transaction details: account activity, payment information, and transaction history.
  • Technical/usage data: IP address, browser/device details, and interaction logs.

2. How Data Is Used

Kayi uses collected data to provide and maintain services, verify identity, process transactions, support customer requests, monitor system usage, improve products, and help prevent fraud and abuse.

  • Service operations and account management
  • Customer communication and support responses
  • Fraud prevention, risk control, and compliance obligations
  • Service improvement and analytics

3. Sharing and Disclosure

Information may be shared with service providers and technology partners that support Kayi's operations, and with regulators or authorities where required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, users, and systems.

  • Operational service providers (hosting, support, payment processing, analytics)
  • Regulatory or legal requests where disclosure is required
  • Business continuity or corporate transaction scenarios, subject to applicable notice requirements

4. Security Measures

Kayi indicates use of technical and organizational controls to help protect personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure, so users should also secure their own credentials and devices.

5. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, users may request access to their data, corrections of inaccurate information, and deletion or restriction in certain circumstances. Users may also withdraw consent where consent is the legal basis for processing.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Kayi may use cookies and similar technologies to improve performance, maintain sessions, understand usage patterns, and optimize user experience.

7. Retention and Policy Updates

Information may be retained for as long as necessary to provide services, satisfy legal and regulatory obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Kayi may revise the Privacy Policy and publish updates on official channels.
