Security

How Kayi protects your account

BVN and NIN Verification

Identity checks using BVN and NIN help protect against impersonation and unauthorized account creation.

Biometrics and Device Checks

Biometric authentication and device-level controls add stronger protection for sensitive transactions.

Password and PIN Controls

Use strong passwords and never share your PIN. Kayi does not request PIN/password details via calls, SMS, or social media.

3DS Card Protection

Card payments use 3D Secure verification to reduce unauthorized card use during online transactions.

Scam Awareness

Stay alert and protect your money

Common Scam Methods

Fraudsters may impersonate staff, send fake links, or request OTP, PIN, password, BVN, or card details.

How to Stay Safe

Only use official channels, verify unexpected messages, and report suspicious activity immediately.

Never share these with anyone:

  • One-Time Passwords (OTP)
  • Card PIN and transaction PIN
  • Internet profile password
  • Full card details and CVV
  • BVN/NIN details in unsolicited chats
