1. Scope and Data Sources

Kayi's data protection / data safety policy describes how customer and user data may be collected and processed across account opening, service use, app/web interactions, cookies, and selected third-party integrations or institutions.

2. Categories of Data

Data categories may include personal identification information, contact information, financial/account details, transaction history, device metadata, usage information, and in some cases location or identity verification data required for service delivery and compliance.

  • Identity and profile data
  • Transaction and financial records
  • Technical and device information
  • Compliance and verification data

3. Purpose of Processing

Kayi may process data for onboarding, service provision, transaction execution, support operations, fraud prevention, risk management, service optimization, and compliance with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Sharing and Transfers

Data may be shared with service providers, contractors, payment infrastructure partners, regulators, law enforcement or authorized agencies where required, and in certain corporate events such as merger or acquisition processes, subject to applicable legal requirements.

5. Data Retention

Data may be retained for as long as needed to fulfill stated purposes, maintain records, meet legal/regulatory requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements or policies.

6. Security Safeguards

Kayi indicates it uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data, which may include encryption, access controls, monitoring, and secure processing practices.

7. Data Subject Rights

Subject to applicable law, users may request access to personal data, correction of inaccuracies, deletion or restriction in certain cases, and may object to certain processing or withdraw consent where relevant.

8. Policy Updates

Kayi may update its data protection policy and publish changes through official channels. Users should review updates periodically to stay informed about how data is handled.

9. Contact

For data protection inquiries, users should use Kayi's official contact channels.
